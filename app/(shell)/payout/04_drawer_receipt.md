## Manual Adjustment Mode

Manual adjustment is controlled by a global toggle placed above the payout fee cards.

When adjustment mode is off:
- fee cards display Amount only
- values are read-only

When adjustment mode is on:
- editable fee cards display:
  - Amount
  - Adjusted Amount
- users may update the adjusted amount for each applicable fee category

The manual adjustment toggle must not appear inside the Bonuses card.
